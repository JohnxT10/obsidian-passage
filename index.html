Obsidian Passage

Congratulations!

You found the exit, but the darkness lingers behind your eyes.

Will you tempt fate again, or has the passage marked you?

How to Play
  • Use arrow keys (or on-screen arrows on mobile/tablet) to move the key through the maze.
  • Select your difficulty, then press Start to descend into the darkness.
  • Switch between Light and Dark mode, but beware what lurks in the shadows.
  • Find the hidden door before the passage claims you.
  • Escape before time runs out... or be trapped forever.
